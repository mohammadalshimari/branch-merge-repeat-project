The story is about an inhibited priestess who is constantly opposing a mystical nun. It starts in a large city in Africa. The crux of the story involves someone writing in a diary. Recovering magical artifacts and knowledge is a major part of the story.

The story opens with a fight scene between the priestess and the nun. The sun is beating down on them as the dry sand of the African city streets gets caught in their eyes. The fight seems to be never ending until a magical artifact appears in the sky.
